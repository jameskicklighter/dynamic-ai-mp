# Small-airframe MIO helpers and inactive A/B/C variant catalog.
# Authored source of truth. Run tools/generate_plane_role_licensing.py --write
# after changing a profile; the generator never rewrites this file.

# Primary mappings follow DAI's normal AI-visible manufacturer policy. Alternate
# mappings are exact-role compatible and are attempted only when the engine says
# the organization is available; several are intentionally hidden from ordinary
# AI selection and therefore remain guarded fallbacks rather than forced choices.
